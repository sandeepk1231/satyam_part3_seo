How to Make the Perfect Pizza Dough

  1. Let the dough thaw at room temperature until soft.
  2. Cut the dough in half (if required) and stretch it into your desired size.
  3. Sprinkle flour on the dough and pizza peel to prevent sticking.
  4. Add your favorite toppings while keeping the base floured.
  5. Slide the dough into your Bertello Pizza Oven and bake to perfection.
  6. Remove the pizza, slice, and enjoy authentic Neapolitan-style pizza!
