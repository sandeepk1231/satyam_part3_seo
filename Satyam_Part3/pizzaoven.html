Versatile Fuel Options

Bertello Pizza Ovens are designed for versatility, allowing users to cook with wood, charcoal, gas, or even a combination of wood and gas simultaneously. This flexibility ensures optimal flavor and cooking conditions tailored to your preference.

Wood

In our pizza oven, the choice of fuel is truly in your hands. Whether you opt for wood kindling, hearty chunks, or convenient pellets, each brings its own distinct character and aroma to the baking process. By embracing these traditional fuel options, our oven ensures that every pizza emerges with that unmistakable wood-fired flavor, transporting your taste buds to an authentic pizzeria experience with every bite.

Charcoal

Our pizza oven is designed to seamlessly accommodate charcoal as a fuel source. For optimal results and a harmonious blend of heat and flavor, we recommend using charcoal as a foundational layer and then layering wood on top. The steady burn of the charcoal maintains the oven's high temperatures, ensuring consistent cooking, while the wood not only sustains the fire but also imparts that sought-after wood-fired flavor to your dishes. This combination of charcoal and wood delivers both the heat and the aromatic essence emblematic of classic wood-fired cuisine.

SimulFIRE Gas + Wood

Our uniquely designed pizza oven offers an unmatched dual-fuel experience, allowing users to cook with gas alone or to harmoniously combine gas and wood as fuel sources. Holding a patent for this innovative feature, we proudly stand as the sole pizza oven in the market that can simultaneously utilize both gas and wood. This dual capability ensures you can easily and conveniently heat the oven with gas to temperatures exceeding 900 degrees Fahrenheit. By adding wood to this equation, you not only maintain the desired temperature, but you also infuse your pizza with the authentic and cherished wood-fired flavor, blending the best of modern convenience and traditional taste.

Bringing authentic pizza-making to your home with Bertello's dual-fuel ovens.
